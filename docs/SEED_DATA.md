# Seed Data and Vibe Prompts

These prompts are the core content strategy to foster the "Chemistry-First" product requirement. The prompts are designed to be emotionally intelligent and low-pressure.

## 1. Onboarding Quick Questions (The "Vibe Definition")
*(Users select predefined answers on sliders or multiple choice)*
- "What energy are you carrying tonight?" (Calm / Chaotic / Overthinking / Bored)
- "What drains your social battery?" (Small talk / Debates / Negativity / Too much excitement)
- "My favorite way to connect is..." (Deep diving into one topic / Rapid fire jokes / Comfortable silence / Trading hot takes)
- "Biggest green flag right now?" (Emotional honesty / Good music taste / Fast replies / Matching humor)

## 2. Chat Icebreakers (In-session prompts)
*(If a text chat goes quiet for 60 seconds, the system injects these as clickable buttons)*
- **Deep Talk**: "What’s something you’ve changed your mind about recently?"
- **Fun**: "What’s a movie opinion you will defend with your life?"
- **Honest Hour**: "What are you overthinking right now?"
- **Flirty/Playful**: "What’s your most specific green flag in a person?"

## 3. Room Themes (Initial Default Rooms)
- `Late-night overthinkers` (Mood: Dim/Purple. Topic: Venting, philosophy)
- `Aux Cord` (Mood: Neon Blue. Topic: Music, album drops, concert stories)
- `The Chill Zone` (Mood: Soft Orange. Topic: Casual, how was your day, low effort chat)
